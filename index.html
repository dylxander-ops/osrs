About Tradingscape

A market analysis terminal for the Old School RuneScape Grand Exchange. Built around the OSRS Wiki real-time prices API with quantitative tools that no existing OSRS flipping tool offers.

What's here

Charts with candlestick/spread/line views and overlay indicators (SMA, EMA, Bollinger Bands, VWAP) plus separate panes for RSI(14) and MACD. Built on TradingView Lightweight Charts.

Screener with five preset profiles plus full custom filters across margin, ROI, volume, and price.

Pair finder for substitute items (bones, logs, ores, herbs, food) with live ratio z-scores. When a pair is at >2σ, the cheap leg is statistically stretched relative to its substitute.

Watchlist persistent across sessions with z-score and 7d change highlighting.

GE tax built in correctly: 2% rate (since 29 May 2025), 5M gp per-item cap, sub-50gp exempt.

Data

All prices come live from prices.runescape.wiki/api/v1/osrs, the OSRS Wiki / RuneLite partnership API. Wiki community members trade items and their fills feed the API. No personal data is collected; your watchlist lives in browser storage only.

Important caveats

This is an analysis tool, not financial or game-strategy advice. OSRS markets are subject to dev-blog announcements, polls, and rebalances that can break any statistical assumption overnight. The 2% tax + 5M cap means displayed margins above 250M gp items overstate effective margin; this tool handles the cap correctly. Always verify a thesis before sizing into it.

For best results: pair this tool's quant signals with reading the latest OSRS news posts and dev blogs at oldschool.runescape.com/news.
